Our Mission

SEA-COMPUTE is a cross-border cloud computing marketplace connecting global small developers with affordable GPU/CPU compute resources. We believe every developer deserves fair access to compute power, regardless of geography or budget.

We aggregate upstream compute capacity and resell it to small developers worldwide through a transparent, monthly-billed marketplace — no long-term contracts, no hidden fees.

Who We Serve

SEA-COMPUTE serves small developers, indie teams, researchers, and startups across Southeast Asia, Latin America, Africa, and other emerging markets who need reliable compute without enterprise-level commitments.

  • Indie game developers rendering assets
