About SEA-COMPUTE

Cross-border cloud computing marketplace for global small developers

Our Vision

We believe that access to production-grade cloud computing should not be a privilege of well-funded enterprises. Individual developers, students, and small teams building the next generation of AI applications, media pipelines, and SaaS products deserve the same pay-as-you-go economics, the same operational simplicity, and the same billing transparency as any Fortune-500 customer.

SEA-COMPUTE exists to close that gap. Our five-year vision is to become the default cross-border compute purchasing channel for the global long-tail of small developers, serving one million active accounts across more than 60 non-sanctioned jurisdictions.

Our Team

SEA-COMPUTE is operated by a lean cross-functional team currently structured around seventeen functional roles covering product, engineering, cross-border operations, compliance, finance, and customer support.

During the current growth phase the operating entity is a natural person; corporate incorporation is planned upon meeting the documented graduation criteria. Team members work under a shared operating discipline: transparent decision logs, single-source-of-truth product requirement documents, and a strict "no-freeze, no-lock, no-delay" customer commitment that overrides any short-term revenue consideration.
