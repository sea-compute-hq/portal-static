Terms of Service

§1 Service Commitment

SEA-COMPUTE guarantees the following service standards:

  • No freezing computing power - Your allocated compute resources remain available throughout your billing period
  • No locking balance - Your account balance is always accessible for withdrawal
  • No freezing withdrawal - Withdrawal requests are processed within 24 hours

§2 Billing & Payment

We offer transparent monthly billing with no hidden fees. You are billed based on actual usage at the end of each month. Payment methods include WorldFirst, PayPal, and bank transfer.

§3 Service Availability

We maintain 99.5% uptime SLA. Scheduled maintenance windows are announced 48 hours in advance.

§4 Acceptable Use

Users must comply with all applicable laws and regulations. Prohibited activities include mining cryptocurrency without authorization, DDoS attacks, and distribution of malicious software.
