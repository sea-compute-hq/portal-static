Frequently Asked Questions

SEA-COMPUTE operates as a lightweight asset middleman during the beta phase. We aggregate upstream capacity (Vast.ai, Lambda, RunPod) and pass the cost savings directly to you. Since we don't control the underlying infrastructure, we cannot offer SLA guarantees in the first month. This is a red-line policy — no exceptions.

GPU compute is a consumable resource — once allocated, the cost is incurred. As a beta platform with zero-margin pricing, we cannot absorb refund costs. We encourage all users to start with the $5 free trial credit to test before committing larger amounts.

Our prices are already 17-28% below upstream providers. We maintain a zero-discount policy to ensure fairness across all beta users. The only exception is the one-time $5 trial credit for beta seats and invite code bonuses — these are acquisition incentives, not price reductions.

First month KYC is simplified (email + phone only). Beta seat holders get priority 24h processing. Full KYC will be required before placing orders after the beta phase.

Your GPU instances run on upstream providers (Vast.ai / Lambda) in Singapore or other Asia-Pacific regions. STANDARD tier SKUs offer Asia data residency. BUDGET tier SKUs may route through P2P nodes — check the SKU card for details.

During the beta phase, payments are processed manually via 万里汇/USDT/支付宝. After you transfer, upload the receipt screenshot and our team will confirm within 24h. Starting D22, we'll switch to Stripe + PingPong for automated payment processing.
