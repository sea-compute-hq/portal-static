Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Data Controller

SEA-COMPUTE is the data controller for all personal information collected through our platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

Data Controller: SEA-COMPUTE
Address: 123 Computing Street, Singapore
Email: privacy@sea-compute.com

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our cross-border cloud computing services:

  • Account information (name, email, payment details)
  • Usage data (compute hours, resource consumption)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)
  • KYC-Lite verification data where required

Subject Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@sea-compute.com.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active account data: retained for the duration of your account
  • After account closure: retained for 2 years for legal and tax compliance
  • Billing records: retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Server logs: retained for 90 days

Target Market Restrictions

SEA-COMPUTE does not provide services to users located in US-sanctioned countries or regions. We strictly comply with all applicable export control and sanctions regulations. Services are not available to users in:

  • Russia
  • Iran
  • DPRK (North Korea)
  • Cuba
  • Syria
  • Other US-sanctioned regions as designated by OFAC

International Data Transfers

As a cross-border service provider, your data may be processed in multiple jurisdictions. We ensure all transfers comply with GDPR Chapter V requirements through Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.
