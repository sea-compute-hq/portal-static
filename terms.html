Terms of Service

Last updated: July 15, 2026

§1 Service Description

SEA-COMPUTE operates a cross-border cloud computing marketplace, providing GPU/CPU compute rental services to global small developers. We aggregate upstream compute capacity and resell it through our platform.

§2 Service Commitment

SEA-COMPUTE guarantees the following service standards to all users:

  • No freezing computing power — Your allocated compute resources remain fully available and operational throughout your active billing period. We will not suspend or throttle your compute without prior notice.
  • No locking balance — Your account balance is always accessible. You may request withdrawal of unused funds at any time.
  • No freezing withdrawal — Withdrawal requests are processed within 24 business hours. We do not impose arbitrary holds on your funds.

§3 Billing & Payment

All services are billed on a monthly basis based on actual usage. You will receive an itemized invoice at the end of each billing cycle. Accepted payment methods include WorldFirst, PayPal, bank transfer (USD/EUR/GBP), and Alipay for qualified accounts.

No long-term contracts are required. You may cancel your services at any time, and you will only be billed for usage up to the cancellation date.

§4 Service Availability

We maintain a 99.5% monthly uptime SLA. Scheduled maintenance windows are announced at least 48 hours in advance via email and platform notification.

§6 Account Termination

We reserve the right to terminate accounts that violate these terms. Upon termination, you will receive 30 days notice to withdraw remaining balance and export your data.

§7 Limitation of Liability

SEA-COMPUTE's total liability shall not exceed the amount you paid in the 12 months preceding the claim. We are not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
